# Project Brief: FitPlate

## Overview
FitPlate is a food delivery platform specializing in healthy, personalized meal plans. The platform connects customers with nutritious meals tailored to their dietary preferences, fitness goals, and nutritional requirements.

## Core Mission
Provide convenient access to healthy, personalized meals through a modern, user-friendly platform that supports multi-language access and comprehensive meal planning.

## Key Objectives
1. Enable users to create personalized meal plans based on their goals (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance, athletic performance)
2. Provide a seamless ordering and delivery experience
3. Offer a comprehensive admin dashboard for managing operations
4. Support multiple languages (Uzbek, Russian, English) for accessibility
5. Implement role-based access control for staff management

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Health-conscious individuals seeking convenient, nutritious meals
- **Secondary**: Fitness enthusiasts with specific nutritional requirements
- **Tertiary**: Busy professionals wanting healthy meal options

## Success Criteria
- Users can complete onboarding and subscribe to meal plans
- Admin staff can effectively manage all aspects of operations
- System supports multi-location delivery operations
- Platform is accessible in multiple languages
